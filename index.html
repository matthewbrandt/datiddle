Scrabble Mode

In this game mode, you will start with 39 points. Each guess will subtract points from your balance based on the total value of the word.

The points are calculated using Scrabble's points system:

  • 1: A,E,I,O,U,L,N,S,T,R
  • 2: D,G
  • 3: B,C,M,P
  • 4: F,H,V,W,Y
  • 5: K
  • 8: J,X
  • 10: Q,Z
